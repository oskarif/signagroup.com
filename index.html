Signature is Signa's wholesale trading and deal management solution for global carriers — designed to take bilateral voice and SMS agreements out of spreadsheets and into a single, trustworthy system.

  • Faster agreements. Create, review and approve deals in one place instead of scattered email threads.
  • Clear economics. Simulate net impact of price changes before you sign. Track live deals with traffic and margin views.
  • Less spreadsheet risk. Shared templates and built-in validation reduce copy‑paste errors and version drift.
  • Built for telecom teams. Destination-based logic, numbering plans and approval workflows help speed up deal creation.
